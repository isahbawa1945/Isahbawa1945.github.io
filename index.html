About Our School

Madarasatul Arkanul Islam Wadirasatul Islamiyya was established on 8th August 2013 with the aim of providing quality Islamic education, sound moral upbringing, and academic excellence. Our mission is to produce students who are knowledgeable, disciplined, and useful to their communities.

📖 Our Mission

To provide quality Islamic education that nurtures knowledge, discipline and good character.

👁 Our Vision

To become one of the leading Islamic learning institutions in Nigeria.

🎯 Our Motto

Knowledge Eliminates Poverty.
