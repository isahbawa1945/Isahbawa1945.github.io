About Our School

Madarasatul Arkanul Islam Wadirasatul Islamiyya was established on 8th August 2013 with the aim of providing quality Islamic education and sound moral upbringing to children and adults.

Our motto is: "Qur'an, Sunnah, Knowledge." We believe knowledge is the greatest investment anyone can acquire.

Our Classes

Beginners Class

A solid foundation where learners begin with Qur'an recitation, Arabic letters, Islamic manners, and basic Islamic knowledge.

Intermediate Class

Students deepen their understanding of Islamic teachings, Arabic reading, and essential religious studies.

Advanced Class

Advanced learning in Qur'an, Fiqh, Hadith, Tauheed, Arabic grammar, and other Islamic sciences.

Tahfiz Class

Special programme for memorization of the Noble Qur'an with proper Tajweed and revision.
